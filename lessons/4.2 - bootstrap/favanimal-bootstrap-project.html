Types of Jellyfish

Moon Jellyfish

Moon jellyfish are one of the most common jellyfish species. They are translucent and have delicate, saucer-shaped bells.

Box Jellyfish

Box jellyfish are known for their venomous tentacles. They have a cube-shaped bell and are found in tropical waters.

Lion's Mane Jellyfish

Lion's Mane jellyfish are the largest known jellyfish. They have long, flowing tentacles and can be found in colder waters.
